The Ultimate AeroPress Technique

This method, developed and tested by coffee expert James Hoffmann, focuses on consistency, simplicity, and a smooth final cup.

  1. Measure and grind. Use 11g of coffee, ground at the finer end of medium. Adjust grind size and dose slightly for darker roasts.
  2. Add coffee and water. Insert a paper filter into the cap (no rinsing needed). Add coffee to the brewer, then pour 200g of freshly boiled water, ensuring all grounds are evenly saturated.
  3. Seal and steep. Start a timer and immediately insert the plunger about 1cm into the top to create a vacuum. Let steep for 2 minutes.
  4. Swirl. Gently swirl the brewer to redistribute the grounds, then wait another 30 seconds.
  5. Press and enjoy. Press slowly and steadily until complete. Serve and enjoy a clean, balanced cup.
